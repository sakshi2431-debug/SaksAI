=== DESIGN PHILOSOPHY ===

I believe in creating experiences that are trustworthy, accessible, and user-centered. My approach emphasizes:

**User-Centered Design:**
I put users at the heart of every design decision, ensuring that solutions address real user needs and pain points through thorough research and testing.

**Design as Care:**
I practice "design as care"—an approach that centers the wellbeing, dignity, and empowerment of users in all design decisions, particularly when working with AI systems.

**Collaborative Approach:**
I believe great design happens through collaboration. I actively contribute, listen, and refine ideas with team members, stakeholders, and users to achieve the best outcomes.

**Research-Driven:**
Every design decision should be backed by research and data. I use various research methodologies to understand users deeply before crafting solutions.

**Ethical AI Design:**
When working with AI systems, I prioritize:
- Building trust with users
- Giving users full control over their data
- Ensuring technology protects and empowers users
- Considering broader societal impacts
- Preserving human dignity and identity

**Accessibility First:**
Accessibility isn't an afterthought—it's a core design principle. I believe in creating inclusive experiences that work for all users, regardless of their abilities.

**Iterative Refinement:**
I embrace iteration and feedback, continuously refining designs based on user testing and stakeholder input to achieve optimal results.
